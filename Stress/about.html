About Us

Purpose

Our mission at Wash Away the Stress is to provide a helpful resource for teens and
adults that are struggling to cope with stress. It is our goal to give people the
opportunity to destress in a fun, yet relaxing manner. Our users are as important
to us as Chicken Nuggets are to McDonalds.




Inspiration

Two of our members are rising seniors and we have been faces with immense stress
due to college applications being due in the next few month. In our summer, we
participated in a Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. This
class taught us how to code and create websites. As our final project, we realized
that stress was a major conflict in al of our lives, so we wanted to create a
website that featured stress relief activities and tips.
